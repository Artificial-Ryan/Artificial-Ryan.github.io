About Artificial Ryan

Artificial Ryan is a multifaceted media company centered around the collaborative creation of artisan goods and various forms of media by Ryan Harp and artificial intelligence tools. It is an experiment in collaborative creativity and a testament to the potential of AI as a tool for artistic expression, guided by the human spirit.

About Ryan Harp

Ryan Harp is the human face of Artificial Ryan, a lone artisan with a digital twist. He is a self-taught designer, a meticulous curator, and a relentless entrepreneur, a man with perpetually ink-stained fingers and a spark in his eyes. Ryan is personally involved in the symphony of production, selecting blank canvases and operating the direct-to-garment printer. He also personally assembles each package, often including a handwritten thank-you note or a small, hand-designed sticker, demonstrating a dedication to quality and customer satisfaction, and adding a personal touch.

Ryan's passion for Artificial Ryan stems from his belief in the power of collaboration between humans and AI. He sees AI as a tool that can be used to enhance human creativity and to create new and exciting forms of art and media.

Etsy Shop & AI Creation

Artificial Ryan predominantly creates print-on-demand objects such as tote bags, towels, and T-shirts. These products are available on the Artificial Ryan Etsy shop. The designs originate from simple prompts fed into AI design software by Ryan. The AI generates a kaleidoscope of visual possibilities based on vast datasets of art, patterns, and trends. Ryan Harp plays a crucial role in refining the AI-generated designs, curating, filtering, tweaking colors, adjusting compositions, and adding subtle, personal details, sometimes including hand-drawn elements or refining AI-generated text.
